About Meal Prep Codex

Welcome to Meal Prep Codex, a platform dedicated to making cooking easier, faster, and more accessible for everyone. Whether you're a seasoned chef or a home cook looking for inspiration, we aim to provide a curated selection of recipes that are both delicious and simple to prepare.

The Story

Meal Prep Codex was built in 2025 as a solo project to explore Django, APIs, and Web Scraping. It started as a way to learn modern web development while solving a real-world problem: making high-quality recipes available for free, without clutter or unnecessary paywalls.

  • Fast & Free Recipes: No subscriptions, no sign-upsâ€”just recipes, instantly available.
  • API for Developers: We provide a structured API with a large collection of recipes, so others can integrate it into their own applications.
  • Original Content: While we aggregate some well-known recipes, we also focus on providing unique and original dishes.

Technology Behind the Project

This platform is built using Django as the backend framework, leveraging APIs and Web Scraping to gather and serve a wide variety of recipes. The goal was to learn how to build a robust and scalable web application while providing something valuable to the community.

Our Mission

We believe that cooking should be simple, fun, and accessible to everyone. Our mission is to create a reliable, ad-free recipe hub where users can quickly find, save, and share meal ideas.
